# DayTrack PWA — Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes)

## Step 1 — Get the files
Download the zip from Claude and unzip it.

## Step 2 — Deploy free with Vercel
1. Go to https://vercel.com and sign up (free)
2. Click "Add New → Project"
3. Click "Upload" and drag the entire `pwa` folder
4. Click Deploy — done. You get a URL like `daytrack.vercel.app`

## Step 3 — Add to iPhone home screen
1. Open the URL in **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name it "DayTrack" → tap Add
5. It now lives on your home screen like a real app ✓

## Step 4 (optional) — AI Insights
1. Get an Anthropic API key at https://console.anthropic.com
2. Open the app → Settings → paste your key
3. Tap "Get AI insight" on the Today screen

## Features
- Wellbeing: sleep, exercise, water, mood, focus (tap bars to log)
- Business: revenue logging + task tracking
- Nutrition: full macro tracking (calories, protein, carbs, fat)
- Daily score ring (0–100)
- 7-day history
- Works offline after first load
- Data saved locally on your device
